Magic of the Crystal Quay

You can use the Magic Types buttons to filter:
Your first click will cause a white glow and filter for attunements with only the desired magic type.
A second click will trigger a red glow and filter out any attunements providing that type of magic.
A third click will reset the filter.

You can also use the Filter Mode button to change filter behaviour:
AND: Only attunements with all selected magic types will be shown.
OR: Attunements with any of the selected magic types will be shown.
AND Primary: Only attunements with all the selected magic types available immediately will be displayed.
OR Primary: Attunements with any of the selected magic types available immediately will be shown.

Two foundational magics create a compound magic, a blend of the two. But opposing types cannot blend and if used together cause a deteriorated growth rate. Opposing trends do not blend together but do not cause deteriorated growth.

Magic Described

Air: A magic of freedom and movement, Air governs wind, space, and direction, excelling in mobility, sensory extension, and the manipulation of the unseen paths between things.

Earth: A magic of restriction and solidity, Earth governs stone, structure, and physical matter, reinforcing durability, shaping terrain, and anchoring the physical world in place.

Fire: A destructive force of heat and release, Fire governs combustion and energy, specialising in rapid change, overwhelming force, and the violent breakdown of what exists.

Water: A constructive force of adaptability and cohesion, Water governs fluidity and balance, enabling control over liquids, pressure, and the binding or dispersal of substances.

Light: A constructive force aligned with freedom, Light governs revelation and presence, bringing what is hidden into focus and strengthening the expression of things as they truly are.

Umbral: A restrictive force of obscurity and distortion, Umbral governs shadow and concealment, specialising in suppression, draining influence, and the warping of what is perceived or present.

Death: A destructive force of endings and decay, Death governs entropy and cessation, eroding vitality, dismantling structure, and bringing all things toward finality.

Life: A constructive force of growth and restoration, Life governs vitality and development, nurturing living systems, reinforcing resilience, and enabling recovery and continuation.

Motion: A force of freedom expressed through action, Motion governs movement, momentum, and temporal flow, accelerating, displacing, and driving change across space and time.

Enhancement: A restrictive force that empowers through reinforcement, Enhancement governs augmentation and intensification, strengthening properties, amplifying effects, and imposing greater function onto what already exists.

Perception: A destructive-leaning force of heightened awareness, Perception governs the senses and their limits, sharpening, extending, or overwhelming physical input to reveal or distort reality.

Mental: A constructive force of knowledge and cognition, Mental governs thought, memory, and understanding, enabling the gathering, refinement, and sharing of knowledge.

Earning Magic

The three spires of the Crystal Quay are, like hourglasses, formed of six pyramids. Each of the pyramids is associated with different virtues, but entering the pyramid of Influence and Unique Resolve typically causes teleportation into another trial at random. Even so, some take the risk on the chance they may receive one of the rare and powerful attunements within.

Change and Curious Acuity Connection and Bright Insight
Expression and Creative Intellect Imagination and Unyielding Will
Strategy and Hard Wisdom Influence and Unique Resolve

Beings of power within the Relative Quay have the ability to alter, change, grant, and remove attunements, and given the unique situation within the Trials of Influence and Unique Resolve, it's associated attunements are sometimes the most treasured gifts these beings can grant.

This means that, outside of intervention by a being of power, only one attunement from each trial can be gained through traditional means.

Taking a Trial

This is the manner by which most gain an attunement, when first entering each pyramid, one can choose to enter a trial. These trials are unique in that they must be tackled alone and in general, the more powerful a person is in their attunements, the harder the trial is to complete.

During the trial, the type of attunement best suiting the persons methods of tackling the challenges within is granted to them in the form of a pearl which they press to their skin. This pearl then becomes an arcane tattoo which appears only under magical senses or at the will of it's owner.

Given the methods of attaining attunements, it is not usually possible to attain more than one from each spire without the intervention of a being of power.

Attunement Marks

Each attunement has it's own design, with each on gaining additional strokes in it's design when the attunement is advanced to a new rank. it's location infers certain benefits:

Mind: Typically seen on the forehead or temple this can technically be placed anywhere on the head, a mind mark allows one to cast spells on themselves with less effort and more subtlety.

Heart: A heart mark is placed anywhere on the chest and grants greater proficiency in channelling a spell with focus and time in order to upscale it's potency and effects.

Lung: A lung mark is placed on the upper back or neck and allows a user to suffuse the air with magic in order to spread the effect of their magic much more widely.

Hand: A hand part can be placed anywhere on the hand, arm, or shoulder, and allows a user to gain a greater degree of accuracy and range by casting with gesture and motion.

Leg: A leg mark can be found on a foot, leg, or hip and grants a greater affinity for casting spells with the medium of touch allowing quicker and more potent abilities in this circumstance.

Two attunements do not coexist in the same place, causing magic burnout and destroying the ability to use magic from the affected area. Should an individual try to place an attunement in a location already occupied the first attunement would be erased unless the individual is able to force the process.

Gaining attunements with opposing magic types can be managed by the location of the body in which they are placed. For example, two attunements, one granting water and another granting fire, if placed on opposing hands would allow the user to grow both attunements at an ordinary rate if they were to exclusively use the relevant hands to use those magics.
